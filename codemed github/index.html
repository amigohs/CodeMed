What Are We?

CodeMed is a premier service dedicated to helping aspiring medical students ace their medical school interviews. We provide comprehensive support through experienced tutors, personalized mock interviews, targeted training sessions, and insightful seminars designed to build confidence and ensure success.

Who are we?

CodeMed was founded in 2024 by B. G., a group of passionate medical students in Sydney, Australia. Recognizing the specific challenges and high stakes of medical school interviews, they decided to create a service solely focused on this niche, offering expert guidance and preparation for our country's future doctors.

Packages

CodeMed offers a range of tailored packages to suit different needs and budgets, including special discounts for student email registrations and free Premium subscription to UNSW students!
